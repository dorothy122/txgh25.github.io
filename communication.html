Computing for Communication

Communication in computing involves the transfer of data over a transmission medium between two or more devices. This process is essential for enabling various applications, such as email, video conferencing, and internet browsing. Effective communication ensures that devices can "talk" to each other, facilitating seamless interactions and data sharing across networks.

Uses of Computing in communication

Computers allow for communication across the globe in a variety of formats such as:

  • Email
  • Call
  • SMS
  • Video Conferencing
  • Collaboration
