Computing in Transportation

From GPS to public transportation, electric cars to rockets, our modern transportaion is filled with computers

GPS

There are many systems in a GPS that require computers, from the information transmission, the display screen, and the user input. However, the most famous use of computation in a GPS is the pathfinding algorithm.

Train

The public transportation sectors has many problems they need to solve. What is the shortest route for one person to reach their destination? How many buses and trains are required on a journey for a variable flow of human traffic? How many stations are required to capture as large a population area as possible? These are difficult problems for human planners, which can be aid easier with the help of computers.
